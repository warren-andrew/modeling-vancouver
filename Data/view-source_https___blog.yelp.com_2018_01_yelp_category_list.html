The complete Yelp business category list

  • John Carroll
  • Wednesday, January 31, 2018 • #English, #Business Owners, #FreeTools

Editor’s note: Did you see the date on this post? It’s a little old, but don’t worry, we updated the category list on April 8, 2020.

If you’re looking for the best categories to describe your business, check out the Yelp Category List. There are more than 1,200 different categories to choose from, and the list keeps on growing.

Why categories are important

Having your business in the right categories helps consumers know what your business is all about. Businesses can be in up to three categories. That means there are millions of potential category combinations. You’ll want to make sure the categories you choose best describe your business.

Updating a business’s categories

If you’re a business owner, it’s easy to update your categories in your Yelp for Business account. If you’re a Yelp consumer, you can always suggest an edit to a business’s categories by clicking edit on the Yelp page. Approval of category changes may take our operations team a few business days.

The Yelp category list

Click on the links to jump to an individual category and all its subcategories.
